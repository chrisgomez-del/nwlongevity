Optional Title

The Northwestern Medicine West Health Accelerator is an initiative to make mental health care more accessible by integrating it into primary care through a Collaborative Care Model (CoCM). Seamless access to mental health care means earlier intervention and more optimized treatments for the patients across the country who need it most.
