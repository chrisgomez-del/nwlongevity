“A pervasive misunderstanding of the nature of psychiatric illness—and the benefits of psychiatric treatment—has impaired our ability to achieve reintegration of care.”

This only continues to foster a misunderstanding of efficacy towards available treatment options. The way forward requires that we build a health system designed to enhance stigma-free identification of patients, and uses effective, integrated clinical partnerships to fully address morbidity and mortality from psychiatric illness.

– Lisa J. Rosenthal, MD, FALCP, DFAPA
